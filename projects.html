Technologies for Inner Development

A collection of software, hardware, cognitive tools, meditation mechanisms, and experimental protocols — each designed to increase awareness, perception, memory, self-regulation, and conscious choice.

Cognitive Training

Trifox / FoxGrid

A visual memory and imagination training game based on reconstructing geometric forms from memory. Designed to strengthen visual working memory, spatial reasoning, pattern recognition, and mental imagery.

01
Engineering Intelligence

Avirlog

A structured engineering decision-log system that captures technical reasoning, design tradeoffs, project knowledge, and decision history. Built to preserve thinking, improve judgment, and accelerate engineering learning.

02
Breath Intelligence

Breath Tracker

A tool for making breathing visible, measurable, and trainable. Designed to help users observe respiratory patterns and develop greater self-regulation through breath awareness.

03
Meditation Device

Water Drop Meditation Device

A physical meditation mechanism using water, rhythm, sound, anticipation, and silence to draw attention into the present moment.

04
Elemental Interfaces

Four Elements Meditation Systems

A family of experimental meditation mechanisms based on earth, water, fire, and air. Each element becomes a sensory interface for attention, embodiment, and contemplation.

Consciousness Protocol

Proof of Silence Blockchain

A speculative blockchain concept where value is linked to intentional stillness, attention, and silence rather than computational waste or financial speculation.
